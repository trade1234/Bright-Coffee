About Us

Bright Coffee Exporter is a green Arabica coffee exporter based in Addis Ababa Ethiopia. Bright Coffee is a registered company under Commercial Registration and Business Licence Proclamation No. 980/2016 of Ethiopia and founded on the principles of bringing fairness and transparency tothe coffee value chain while satisfying customer demand.
The name 'Bright' is name from the inspiration of the bright characteristics of coffee. Our Company specializes in coffee sourced from Yirgacheffe, Sidamo, guji, Lekempti and Djimma, names that epitomise fine Arabica coffee. The passion for fine coffee is coupled with consistent reinvestment not only in improved infrastructure, technology and processes, but Bright Coffee also invests 10% of its annual profits into social responsibility programs such as building schools and providing clean water to the communities it serves.
Bright coffee's philosophy is based on sustainablity, serving with consistancy, integrity and Excellence, and traceability are our core values.
